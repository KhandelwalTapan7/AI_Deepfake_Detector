AURORA

Deepfake Intelligence Platform · v3.0

Aurora is a custom-trained neural network detection system built to expose AI-generated and manipulated imagery. Trained on a balanced three-class dataset with up to 5,000 images per class, the model achieves 95.27% validation accuracy using an EfficientNet-B0 backbone with cosine annealing and differential fine-tuning.
